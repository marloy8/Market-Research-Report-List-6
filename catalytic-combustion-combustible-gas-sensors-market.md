<p><p>Catalytic combustion combustible gas sensors are vital for detecting flammable gases in various environments. The market can be categorized into two main types: diffusion and inhalation sensors. Diffusion sensors passively allow gas to enter through a membrane, relying on concentration gradients for detection. In contrast, inhalation sensors actively draw gas samples into the sensor using a pump, ensuring rapid and accurate readings. Both types play critical roles in safety monitoring across industries such as petrochemicals, manufacturing, and construction.</p></p>
