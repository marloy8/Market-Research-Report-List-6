<p><strong>The Energy and Sport Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy and sports drinks market is categorized into three main types: hypotonic, isotonic, and hypertonic sports drinks. Hypotonic drinks have lower osmolarity, quickly rehydrating the body and providing a small amount of carbs, ideal for light exercise. Isotonic drinks match the body’s osmolarity, supplying hydration and energy, making them suitable for moderate to intense workouts. Hypertonic drinks contain high sugar concentrations, offering concentrated energy, best used for recovery after extended physical activities. Each type caters to specific hydration and energy needs during sports.</p></p>
