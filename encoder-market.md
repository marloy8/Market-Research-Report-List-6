<p><strong>The Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Encoder</li><li>Linear Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The encoder market is primarily segmented into rotary and linear encoders. Rotary encoders convert the angular position of a shaft into a digital signal, making them essential for applications requiring precise rotational measurements, like robotics and automation. Linear encoders, on the other hand, measure the position of an object along a straight path, widely used in CNC machines and industrial automation for precise linear motion control. Both types are crucial in enhancing accuracy and efficiency in various technological fields.</p></p>
