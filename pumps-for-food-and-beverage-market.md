<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pumps for the food and beverage market primarily include centrifugal and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, making them ideal for transferring liquids at high flow rates with low viscosity, such as water or juices. Conversely, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of liquid and forcing it through the discharge, making them suitable for thicker substances like sauces and creams. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining efficiency and hygiene in food processing.</p></p>
