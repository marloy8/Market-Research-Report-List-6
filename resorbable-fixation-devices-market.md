<p><p>The resorbable fixation devices market includes absorbable bone plates, absorbable screws, and other specialized products. Absorbable bone plates provide stability for fractured bones and gradually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for surgical removal. Absorbable screws are used to secure bone fragments and degrade over time, promoting natural healing. Other devices may include pins, staples, or scaffolds, which serve specific orthopedic applications. These innovations enhance patient comfort and reduce complications associated with permanent implants, thereby improving overall recovery.</p></p>
