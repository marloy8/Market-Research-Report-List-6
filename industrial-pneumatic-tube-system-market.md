<p><p>The Industrial Pneumatic Tube System market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Phase and Three Phase Pneumatic Tube Systems. Single Phase Pneumatic Tube Systems utilize a single air stream to transport items, offering simplicity and efficiency for short-distance conveyance. In contrast, Three Phase Pneumatic Tube Systems incorporate multiple air streams, allowing for the simultaneous transportation of different materials or larger volumes over greater distances. This enhances operational flexibility and increases throughput in various industrial applications, including healthcare and logistics.</p></p>
