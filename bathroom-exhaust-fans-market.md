<p><p>Bathroom exhaust fans come in various types, including centrifugal exhaust fans, which use a rotating impeller to efficiently move air and are ideal for larger spaces. Inline centrifugal exhaust fans are installed within ductwork, providing unobtrusive ventilation while maximizing air movement. Radial exhaust fans have a design that directs airflow radially, often used in smaller applications. Additionally, the "other" category includes specialized or hybrid fans tailored for unique bathroom environments or specific ventilation needs, ensuring effective moisture and odor control.</p></p>
