<p><ul><li>Air Entrained Agent</li><li>Swelling Agent</li><li>Waterproof Agent</li><li>Antifreeze</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lignosulfonate-based concrete admixtures enhance performance in various applications. Air entraining agents introduce microscopic air bubbles, improving freeze-thaw resistance and workability. Swelling agents expand upon hydration, compensating for shrinkage and enhancing durability. Waterproof agents reduce permeability, preventing water ingress and increasing longevity. Antifreeze agents lower the freezing point of concrete, enabling placements in cold conditions, ensuring strength development despite low temperatures. Together, these admixtures optimize concrete properties for diverse environmental conditions and project requirements.</p></p>
