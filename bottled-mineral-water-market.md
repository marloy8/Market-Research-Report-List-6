<p><p>The bottled mineral water market comprises two main types: natural mineral water and artificial mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced from underground springs, rich in minerals and trace elements, requiring minimal processing to retain its inherent properties. In contrast, artificial mineral water is created by infusing purified water with added minerals to achieve a desired composition, often replicating the taste and health benefits of natural varieties. Both types cater to consumer preferences for health, hydration, and taste.</p></p>
