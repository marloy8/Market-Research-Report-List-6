<p><p>The India rubber market includes various types like Ribbed Smoked Sheet (RSS), Technically Specified Rubber (TSR), Latex, and others. RSS is natural rubber processed into sheets, smoked for preservation, and used in tires and industrial products. TSR is standardized rubber with specific technical properties, preferred for consistency in manufacturing. Latex is liquid rubber used in gloves, adhesives, and foam products. Other types include specialty rubbers for niche applications. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs, driving India's rubber industry.</p></p>
