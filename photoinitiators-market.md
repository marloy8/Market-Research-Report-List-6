<p><strong>The Photoinitiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cationic Photoinitiator</li><li>Free Radical Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoinitiators market is primarily categorized into two types: cationic and free radical photoinitiators. Cationic photoinitiators initiate polymerization through cationic reactions, commonly used in applications like coatings and adhesives due to their excellent thermal stability and resistance to moisture. Free radical photoinitiators, on the other hand, generate free radicals upon exposure to light, facilitating polymerization in various materials, including inks and paints. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and performance of photopolymerization processes across multiple industries.</p></p>
