<p><strong>The Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Catheter</li><li>Pigtail-curled Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter market comprises two main types: straight catheters and pigtail-curled catheters. Straight catheters feature a simple design for direct insertion into the peritoneal cavity, promoting efficient dialysis fluid exchange. In contrast, pigtail-curled catheters include a coiled tip that helps minimize the risk of malposition and improves stability within the abdominal cavity. Both types are essential for effective peritoneal dialysis, offering solutions tailored to patient needs and enhancing treatment outcomes in managing kidney failure.</p></p>
