<p><ul><li>Non Homogeneous</li><li>Homogeneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rhodium Carbon Catalyst market can be divided into two primary types: homogeneous and non-homogeneous catalysts. Homogeneous catalysts involve rhodium dispersed within the same phase as the reactants, providing uniformity and ease of handling. Conversely, non-homogeneous catalysts consist of rhodium in a different phase, usually solid, allowing for simpler separation post-reaction but potentially leading to lower reaction rates. Both types are utilized for various industrial applications, including chemical synthesis and environmental processes, each offering distinct advantages and limitations.</p></p>
