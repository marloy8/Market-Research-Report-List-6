<p><strong>The Biophotonic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Sensor</li><li>Extrinsic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biophotonic sensor market consists of two primary types: intrinsic and extrinsic sensors. Intrinsic sensors measure biological signals directly within the biological medium, utilizing the properties of the biological materials themselves for detection and analysis. In contrast, extrinsic sensors involve the application of external devices or markers to interact with the biological system, facilitating measurement and observation of physiological parameters. Both types play critical roles in applications like medical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
