<p><p>Dichroic filters are specialized optical filters that reflect certain wavelengths of light while allowing others to pass through. The market is typically segmented into two primary types: single-phase filters and three-phase filters. Single-phase filters are designed to manage light in simpler applications and are often used in basic lighting systems. In contrast, three-phase filters are more complex and suitable for advanced lighting setups, such as in professional photography or theatrical lighting, where precise color control is essential.</p></p>
