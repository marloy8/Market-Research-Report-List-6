<p><ul><li>Digital Type</li><li>Analog Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optical pressure sensors are categorized into two main types: digital and analog. Digital type sensors convert pressure measurements into a digital signal, allowing for precise data transmission and easy integration with digital systems. They are often preferred for applications demanding high accuracy. In contrast, analog type sensors output continuous voltage or current signals proportional to pressure changes. These sensors are typically simpler and can be more cost-effective, making them suitable for various industrial applications where high precision is less critical.</p></p>
