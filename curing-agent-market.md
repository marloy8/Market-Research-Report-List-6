<p><p>The curing agent market comprises various types used to enhance the properties of resins and polymers. Epoxy curing agents, such as polyamines and anhydrides, facilitate strong bonding in adhesives and coatings. Amides improve flexibility and heat resistance, while silanes enhance adhesion and moisture resistance. Isocyanates are crucial for polyurethane formulations, offering durability. Aziridines are valued for their low toxicity and exceptional adhesion. Other agents include phenolic and dicyandiamide, catering to diverse applications across industries, including construction and automotive.</p></p>
