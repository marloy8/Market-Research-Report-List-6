<p><p>The Titanium Metal Powder Market comprises two main types: High Purity Titanium Metal Powder (HPTP) and Alloyed Titanium Metal Powder (ATP). HPTP is characterized by minimal contaminants and is primarily used in aerospace and medical applications, where purity is crucial. ATP, on the other hand, includes titanium mixed with other elements to enhance specific properties like strength and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for various industrial uses, including automotive and marine sectors. Both types are essential for advancing technology in their respective fields.</p></p>
