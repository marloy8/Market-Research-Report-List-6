<p><p>The Alnico market is primarily divided into Sintered AlNiCo and Cast AlNiCo. Sintered AlNiCo is produced by compacting and heating powdered alloys, offering cost-effectiveness, uniform magnetic properties, and suitability for small, complex shapes. It is widely used in sensors, instruments, and small motors. Cast AlNiCo, created by melting and casting alloys, provides superior magnetic strength and thermal stability, ideal for high-performance applications like aerospace, automotive, and industrial motors. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing cost, performance, and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
