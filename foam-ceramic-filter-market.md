<p><ul><li>Alumina Foam Ceramic Filters</li><li>SiC Foam Ceramic Filters</li><li>Zirconia Foam Ceramic Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foam ceramic filters are porous materials used in metal casting to remove impurities and improve metal quality. **Alumina foam ceramic filters** are made from aluminum oxide, offering high thermal stability and resistance to corrosion, ideal for aluminum and non-ferrous metals. **SiC foam ceramic filters**, composed of silicon carbide, provide exceptional strength and thermal shock resistance, suitable for high-temperature applications like steel casting. **Zirconia foam ceramic filters**, made from zirconium dioxide, excel in extreme temperatures and aggressive environments, often used in superalloy casting. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
