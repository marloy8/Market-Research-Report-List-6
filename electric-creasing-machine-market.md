<p><p>Electric creasing machines come in two main types: tabletop and vertical. Tabletop machines are compact and portable, making them ideal for small businesses or individuals with limited space. They are easy to use and operate on a flat surface. Vertical machines, on the other hand, are larger and more heavy-duty, designed for high-volume production in commercial settings. They have a vertical feeding system, allowing for faster and more efficient creasing of larger quantities of paper. Both types offer precise and professional creasing results for a variety of applications.</p></p>
