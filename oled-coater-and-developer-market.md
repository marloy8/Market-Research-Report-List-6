In contrast, OLED developers are responsible for patterning these layers, enabling the creation of vivid images and colors on screens. Together, they play a crucial role in enhancing display performance, efficiency, and quality, serving diverse sectors including consumer electronics, automotive displays, and flexible screen technology.</p></p>
