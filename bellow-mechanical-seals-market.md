<p><p>The Bellow Mechanical Seals market is categorized into two main types: Pusher Type Seals and Non-Pusher Seals. Pusher Type Seals utilize a dynamic O-ring that maintains contact with the sealing face, enabling better compensation for wear and thermal expansion. In contrast, Non-Pusher Seals rely on a fixed sealing mechanism without the use of dynamic components, offering simplicity and reliability in specific applications. These distinct designs cater to varying environmental demands and operational conditions within industrial settings.</p></p>
