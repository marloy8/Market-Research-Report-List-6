<p><p>The Polypropylene Woven Sacks market is divided into two main types: laminated and non-laminated sacks. Laminated polypropylene woven sacks have a protective layer that enhances moisture resistance, durability, and aesthetic appeal, making them suitable for packaging high-value products. In contrast, non-laminated polypropylene woven sacks are more economical and breathable, commonly used for bulk packaging of agricultural products, construction materials, and other items where exposure to moisture is less critical. Both types cater to varying customer needs and applications.</p></p>
