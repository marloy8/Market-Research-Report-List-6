<p><p>Telecom market types include RF-Based, NB-IoT, and LPWAN, each catering to specific connectivity needs. RF-Based systems utilize radio frequency to establish wireless communication, making them suitable for short-range applications. NB-IoT (Narrowband Internet of Things) provides low-power, wide-area connectivity for devices requiring infrequent data transmission. LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network) encompasses various technologies that enable long-range communication with minimal energy consumption, ideal for IoT deployments in urban and remote areas, driving efficient data exchange and device management.</p></p>
