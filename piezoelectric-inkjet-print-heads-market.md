<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Inkjet Print-heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet</li><li>Random Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric inkjet print-head market is primarily segmented into two types: continuous inkjet (CIJ) and random inkjet. Continuous inkjet systems involve a steady stream of ink droplets, allowing for high-speed printing suitable for large-scale applications, such as packaging. In contrast, random inkjet, or drop-on-demand printing, selectively ejects ink droplets only when needed, making it ideal for high-resolution prints and variable data applications. Each technology serves distinct industrial needs, catering to different printing requirements and environments.</p></p>
