<p><strong>The Flow Chemistry Reaction System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR)</li><li>Plug Flow Reactors (PFR)</li><li>Micro Reactor Systems (MRT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow chemistry reaction systems can be categorized into three main types: Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR), Plug Flow Reactors (PFR), and Micro Reactor Systems (MRT). CSTRs provide continuous mixing of reactants, PFRs allow for precise control of residence time and reaction kinetics, and MRTs offer high surface area-to-volume ratios for efficient heat and mass transfer. Each type of system has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the flow chemistry market.</p></p>
