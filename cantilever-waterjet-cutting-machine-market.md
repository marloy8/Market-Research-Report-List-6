<p><p>The Cantilever Waterjet Cutting Machine market is segmented into two main types: Pressurized Water and Abrasive Mixture. Pressurized Water systems utilize high-pressure streams of water for cutting softer materials such as rubber or foam, offering high precision with minimal kerf. In contrast, Abrasive Mixture systems incorporate abrasive particles into the waterjet, allowing for the cutting of harder materials like metal and stone. This distinction enables diverse applications across industries, catering to specific material needs and cutting requirements.</p></p>
