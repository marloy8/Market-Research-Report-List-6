<p><strong>The MRI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting Type MRI Equipment</li><li>Permanent-magnet Type MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: superconducting and permanent-magnet MRI systems. Superconducting MRI equipment utilizes superconducting magnets to generate strong magnetic fields, allowing for high-resolution imaging and extensive diagnostic capabilities, making it prevalent in hospitals. In contrast, permanent-magnet MRI systems use permanent magnets to create the magnetic field, offering a more compact, cost-effective solution with lower operational costs, ideal for smaller facilities and outpatient settings. Each type serves specific clinical needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
