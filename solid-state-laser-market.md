<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Solid-State Laser</li><li>Pulsed Solid State Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid-state laser market comprises two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) solid-state lasers and Pulsed solid-state lasers. CW solid-state lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring steady output, such as materials processing and medical devices. In contrast, pulsed solid-state lasers produce short bursts of laser light, providing high peak power for applications like laser cutting, drilling, and precise material analysis. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial, medical, and scientific applications.</p></p>
