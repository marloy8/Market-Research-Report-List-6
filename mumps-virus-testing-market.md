<p><strong>The Mumps Virus Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Culture/Immunofluorescence</li><li>Semi-Quantitative Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay</li><li>Semi-Quantitative Chemiluminescent Immunoassay</li><li>Qualitative Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mumps Virus Testing Market includes several testing methodologies. Cell culture/immunofluorescence techniques confirm the presence of the virus using live samples and fluorescent markers. Semi-quantitative enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISAs) measure antibody concentrations, providing insight into infection status. Semi-quantitative chemiluminescent immunoassays utilize light-emitting reactions to quantify antibodies. Qualitative real-time polymerase chain reaction (RT-PCR) detects the viral genetic material, offering rapid and specific identification of mumps infections. Each method varies in sensitivity, specificity, and application in clinical settings.</p></p>
