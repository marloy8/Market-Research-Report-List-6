<p><p>Insulated Panel Systems (IPS) are construction materials designed to offer thermal insulation and structural integrity. These panels typically consist of a rigid foam core enclosed between two layers of thin metal, which provide enhanced energy efficiency in buildings. The growing awareness of sustainability and energy efficiency is driving the increased adoption of IPS in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p><p>The market for Insulated Panel Systems is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.8% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in manufacturing technologies, such as improved foam density and bonding techniques, are enhancing the performance of these panels.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards eco-friendly materials, with manufacturers focusing on recyclable components and low-emission products. Furthermore, the demand for cold storage solutions in industries like food and pharmaceuticals is propelling the adoption of insulated panels.
