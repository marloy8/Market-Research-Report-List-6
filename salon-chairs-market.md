<p><p>The salon chairs market features two primary types: electric reclining salon chairs and hydraulic reclining salon chairs. Electric reclining chairs use an electric motor for effortless adjustments in height and seating position, offering enhanced comfort and convenience for stylists and clients. In contrast, hydraulic reclining chairs utilize a hydraulic pump system for manual height adjustments, known for their durability and stability. Both types cater to different salon needs, balancing comfort and functionality to improve the client experience.</p></p>
