<p><p>The Adhesiveless Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) market encompasses two main types: single-sided and double-sided non-adhesive FCCL. Single-sided non-adhesive FCCL features copper on one side and is primarily used in applications where space and weight savings are essential, such as in mobile devices. In contrast, double-sided non-adhesive FCCL offers copper layers on both sides, enhancing conductivity and flexibility, making it suitable for complex circuit designs in advanced electronics, including automotive and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
