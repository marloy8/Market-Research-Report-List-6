<p><p>Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) splitters are essential components in fiber optic networks, used to distribute optical signals. The 1N type refers to a splitter that divides a single input signal into N output fibers, making it ideal for point-to-multipoint applications. The 2N type, on the other hand, splits the input signal into multiple outputs, typically used for larger systems needing greater distribution capacity. Both types enhance network efficiency and flexibility in telecommunications and data transmission infrastructures.</p></p>
