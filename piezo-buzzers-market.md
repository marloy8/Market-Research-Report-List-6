<p><ul><li>Active Piezo Buzzer</li><li>Passive Piezo Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezo buzzers market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers generate sound when an electrical signal is applied directly, usually containing an integrated oscillator, making them easy to use for basic sound applications. In contrast, passive piezo buzzers require an external signal or waveform to produce sound, allowing for more complex tones and frequencies. Both types are widely utilized in consumer electronics, alarms, and industrial applications for alerting and signaling purposes.</p></p>
