<p><ul><li>Organic</li><li>Inorganic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiblock additive market is categorized into organic and inorganic types based on the chemical composition and source of the additives used to prevent adhesion in packaging materials. Organic antiblock additives, often derived from natural sources or synthetic polymers, improve surface properties without affecting transparency. In contrast, inorganic antiblock additives, typically mineral-based, enhance performance by providing physical barriers to block adhesion while maintaining clarity. Both types are essential in optimizing packaging efficiency and product quality across various industries.</p></p>
