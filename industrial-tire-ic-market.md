<p><strong>The Industrial Tire (IC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial tire market consists primarily of bias tires and radial tires, each serving different applications. Bias tires feature layers of fabric that crisscross, providing flexibility and durability, making them suitable for rough terrain and heavy loads. Conversely, radial tires utilize perpendicular layers, offering better fuel efficiency, improved traction, and a smoother ride. This design makes them ideal for applications requiring high-speed operation and superior performance on varied surfaces. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs across sectors like construction and agriculture.</p></p>
