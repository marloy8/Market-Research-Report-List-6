<p><p>Flexible films are essential in packaging and come in various types, each serving distinct applications. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films are known for their clarity and moisture resistance, making them ideal for food packaging. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) films offer excellent sealability and flexibility, suitable for snack packages. Polyester films provide high strength and durability, commonly used in labels and product wraps. Other flexible film types include polyethylene and biodegradable films, catering to specific packaging needs and sustainability goals.</p></p>
