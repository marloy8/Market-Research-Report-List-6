<p><strong>The Polyacrylamide Copolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Ionic Polyacrylamide (PAMN)</li><li>Anionic Polyacrylamide (APAM)</li><li>Cationic Polyacrylamide (CPAM)</li><li>Amphoteric Polyacrylamide (PAM-CA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyacrylamide Copolymer market comprises four primary types: Non-Ionic Polyacrylamide (PAMN), which is neutral and widely used in various applications; Anionic Polyacrylamide (APAM), characterized by its negative charge for enhanced flocculation and improved clarity; Cationic Polyacrylamide (CPAM), positively charged and effective in wastewater treatment and paper manufacturing; and Amphoteric Polyacrylamide (PAM-CA), which exhibits both positive and negative characteristics, allowing versatility in diverse industrial applications. Each type serves specific functions based on their charge properties.</p></p>
