<p><p>The Platform Screen Door System market is segmented into Full Height and Half Height types. Full Height screen doors extend from floor to ceiling, enhancing safety, climate control, and reducing noise by preventing debris and accidents on tracks. Conversely, Half Height doors cover only part of the platform, allowing for air circulation and visibility while still offering some safety benefits. Both types cater to different transit environments and requirements, depending on factors like passenger volume and urban design.</p></p>
