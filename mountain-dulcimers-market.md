<p><p>Mountain Dulcimers are available in two main market types based on the construction material: All Solid Wood and Laminated Wood. All Solid Wood dulcimers are crafted from high-quality solid wood, providing a rich and resonant tone. These instruments typically cater to more professional players and are known for their superior sound quality and craftsmanship. On the other hand, Laminated Wood dulcimers are made from layers of wood pressed together, offering a more affordable option for beginners or casual players. While they may not have the same tonal richness as solid wood dulcimers, they still provide a satisfactory playing experience.</p></p>
