<p><strong>The Scientific CMOS (sCMOS) Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Illuminated</li><li>Back Illuminated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scientific CMOS (sCMOS) cameras are divided into two main types: front illuminated and back illuminated. Front illuminated cameras have the sensor situated on the front side of the device, allowing for better quantum efficiency but can suffer from increased noise due to light blocking components. On the other hand, back illuminated cameras have the sensor located on the backside, which results in improved sensitivity and lower noise levels. Both types cater to different user requirements, offering unique advantages in various scientific applications.</p></p>
