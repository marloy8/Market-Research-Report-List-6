<p><strong>The Nasolacrimal Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocanalicular Stents</li><li>Bicanalicular Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nasolacrimal stents market is categorized into two primary types: monocanalicular and bicanalicular stents. Monocanalicular stents are designed to traverse a single canaliculus, offering a simpler solution for canalicular obstruction. In contrast, bicanalicular stents involve both canaliculi, providing enhanced drainage capabilities and stability for more complex conditions. The choice between these stent types depends on the severity of the obstruction and the specific anatomical considerations, impacting patient outcomes and satisfaction in ophthalmic treatments.</p></p>
