<p><p>The coating dryer market comprises three primary types: liquid driers, oil driers, and other variants. Liquid driers enhance the drying speed of water-based coatings through the evaporation of solvents. Oil driers, commonly used in oil-based paints, facilitate quicker surface drying by promoting oxidation. Other types encompass specialty driers designed for specific applications, including additives that optimize drying performance and improve product stability. Each type caters to distinct coating formulations and drying needs in various industries.</p></p>
