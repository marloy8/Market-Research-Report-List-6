<p><p>The Atomic Force Microscope (AFM) probe market consists of three primary types: silicon, silicon nitride, and diamond probes. Silicon probes are widely used for their cost-effectiveness and mechanical stability, making them suitable for various applications. Silicon nitride probes offer enhanced elasticity and reduced sensitivity to temperature changes, providing better imaging quality. Diamond probes, known for their exceptional hardness and chemical stability, are ideal for high-resolution imaging of hard materials and demanding environments, although they tend to be more expensive.</p></p>
