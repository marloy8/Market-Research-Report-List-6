<p><p>A Continuous Fiber Laser is a type of laser that generates a continuous beam of light through the amplification of light by stimulated emission in optical fibers. These lasers are known for their high efficiency, compact size, and capability to deliver high power output for industrial applications such as cutting, welding, and marking. Their versatility makes them ideal for various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p><p>The Continuous Fiber Laser Market is experiencing robust growth, with expectations to expand at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
