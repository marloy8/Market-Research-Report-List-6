<p><p>The X-Ray Stretchers market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electrical stretchers. Pneumatic stretchers utilize air pressure for lifting and positioning patients, offering adjustable height and easy maneuverability. Hydraulic stretchers rely on a hydraulic lifting mechanism, providing stable support and precise height adjustments. Electrical stretchers feature motorized systems that allow for effortless elevation and positioning, enhancing user convenience and efficiency. Each type is designed to improve patient transfer and comfort during X-ray examinations while catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
