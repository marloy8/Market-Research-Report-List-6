<p><p>On-ear headphones are categorized into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired headphones connect directly to devices via a cable, offering stable audio quality and no need for batteries. They are often favored for their reliability and lower cost. In contrast, wireless headphones use Bluetooth technology for a cable-free experience, enhancing convenience and portability. They typically feature built-in rechargeable batteries, allowing for more freedom of movement but may require periodic charging. Each type caters to different user preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
