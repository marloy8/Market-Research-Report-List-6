<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inductive Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Inductive Type</li><li>Mutual Inductive Type</li><li>Eddy Current Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive sensors are categorized into three main types based on their operating principles. Self inductive sensors utilize a coil to generate an electromagnetic field, detecting nearby metal objects through changes in inductance. Mutual inductive sensors rely on two coils, measuring the variation in mutual inductance as an object approaches. Eddy current sensors operate by inducing currents in conductive materials, allowing precise measurement of distance or presence. Each type serves diverse applications in automation, automotive, and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
