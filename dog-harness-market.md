<p><p>Dog harness market offers various types to cater to different needs. Back-clip harnesses are designed for dogs that need more control, as the leash attaches on the back, reducing pulling. Front-clip harnesses are great for training and redirecting a dog's attention, as the leash attaches on the chest, discouraging pulling. Dual-clip harnesses provide versatility by offering both front and back attachment points, allowing for different control options. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of dog owners.</p></p>
