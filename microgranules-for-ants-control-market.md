<p><p>Microgranules for ant control come in three primary market types: scattering, spray, and watering. Scattering involves spreading dry microgranules over infested areas, allowing ants to ingest the bait. Spray formulations deliver active ingredients in a liquid form, targeting ants directly or the surfaces they traverse. Watering involves dissolving microgranules in water to create a solution that penetrates the soil, offering a systemic approach to control ants within nests. Each method caters to different application preferences and environmental conditions.</p></p>
