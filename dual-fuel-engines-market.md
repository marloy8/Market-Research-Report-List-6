<p><ul><li>Four-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines</li><li>Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual-fuel engines operate on two types of fuel, typically a combination of diesel and natural gas, allowing for greater efficiency and reduced emissions. The Four-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engine features a cycle where each power stroke occurs every four strokes of the piston, offering better fuel flexibility and smoother operation. In contrast, Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines complete a power stroke every two strokes of the piston, delivering higher power density and efficiency, making them suitable for marine and industrial applications.</p></p>
