<p><p>Fire & Allied Insurance encompasses two primary market types: Fixed Assets Insurance and Current Assets Insurance. In contrast, Current Assets Insurance covers short-term assets like inventory and receivables, safeguarding against potential losses due to fire damage. Both insurance types are crucial for businesses to mitigate financial risks and maintain operational stability in the face of unforeseen events.</p></p>
