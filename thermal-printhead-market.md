<p><p>The thermal printhead market is divided into two main types: thick film thermal printheads and thin film thermal printheads. In contrast, thin film printheads feature a more delicate construction, offering high resolution and better heat transfer for precise printing needs. Both types cater to various industries, including retail, logistics, and manufacturing, based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
