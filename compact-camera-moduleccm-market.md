<p><p>The Compact Camera Module (CCM) market includes two primary types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high-quality imaging and low noise but can consume more power. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integrated functionalities, making them widely used in modern devices like smartphones. As technology advances, the demand for CMOS modules is increasing due to their versatility and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
