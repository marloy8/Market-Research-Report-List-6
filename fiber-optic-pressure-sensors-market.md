<p><p>The Fiber Optic Pressure Sensors market includes several types based on different modulation techniques. Intensity Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors measure pressure by changes in light intensity, while Phase Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors detect pressure variations through phase shifts of light. Wavelength Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors utilize changes in light wavelength to gauge pressure, and Polarization Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors rely on shifts in light polarization. Each type is suited for specific applications, offering advantages in sensitivity, accuracy, and environmental resilience.</p></p>
