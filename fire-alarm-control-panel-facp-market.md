<p><p>Fire Alarm Control Panels (FACP) are essential in detecting and managing fire emergencies. There are two primary market types: conventional and addressable systems. Conventional systems categorize detectors into zones, triggering alarms based on area identification, making them cost-effective for smaller structures. Addressable systems assign unique addresses to each device, allowing for precise location identification of a fire source, enhancing response times and safety in larger or complex buildings. Both types are vital for effective fire safety management.</p></p>
