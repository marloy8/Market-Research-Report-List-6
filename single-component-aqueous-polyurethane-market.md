<p><strong>The Single-component Aqueous Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Single-component Aqueous Polyurethane Market is primarily categorized into two types: Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion (APD) and Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion (APE). APD is a water-based system that offers superior flexibility and adhesion, commonly used in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, APE consists of polymer droplets dispersed in water, providing excellent stability and ease of application. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives to solvent-based systems, catering to various industries, including automotive, construction, and textiles, emphasizing sustainability and performance.</p></p>
