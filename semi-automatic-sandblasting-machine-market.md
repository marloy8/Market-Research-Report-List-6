<p><p>The semi-automatic sandblasting machine market is segmented into dry and wet types. Dry sandblasting involves using abrasive materials propelled by compressed air, ideal for surface preparation and coating removal, offering quick drying times. In contrast, wet sandblasting combines water with the abrasive material, minimizing dust and improving visibility, making it suitable for delicate surfaces and reducing the risk of surface damage. Each type caters to specific applications and environmental considerations, influencing user preferences in various industries.</p></p>
