<p><p>The automotive glass encapsulation market comprises several materials, primarily including PVC, PUR, TPE, and others. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers rigidity and cost-effectiveness, making it a popular choice. PUR (Polyurethane) provides flexibility and superior adhesion, enhancing sealing properties. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) combines the benefits of rubber and plastic, offering excellent weather resistance and durability. Other materials may include silicone and EPDM, which cater to specialized applications. These materials facilitate the integration of glass components in vehicles, improving aesthetics and performance.</p></p>
