<p><p>Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDR) are sophisticated instruments used to detect and analyze faults in fiber optic cables. By sending pulses of light into a fiber, an OTDR measures the time and intensity of the light that is reflected back, allowing for precise evaluation of the fiber's performance and identification of any issues along its length.</p><p>The OTDR market is poised for significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for fiber optic networks in telecommunications, data centers, and broadband applications.
