<p><p>A limit switch is an electromechanical device that detects the presence or position of an object, often used in automation and control applications. When an object pushes the switch, it activates or deactivates machinery, providing critical feedback for various industrial processes. Limit switches are widely employed in sectors like manufacturing, automotive, robotics, and aerospace, ensuring accuracy in machinery operations and safety measures.</p><p>The Limit Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period.
