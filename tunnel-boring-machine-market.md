<p><strong>The Tunnel Boring Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Ground TBMs</li><li>Hard Rock TBMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) market primarily consists of two types: Soft Ground TBMs and Hard Rock TBMs. Soft Ground TBMs are designed for excavating through softer geological formations such as clay, silt, and sand, utilizing adaptable mechanisms to manage ground pressure and water ingress. In contrast, Hard Rock TBMs are engineered for penetrating tough, solid rock, employing robust cutting heads and powerful thrust systems. Together, these machines cater to various tunneling projects, including transportation, sewage, and utility infrastructure.</p></p>
