<p><p>Back grinding tapes are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, used to protect and support wafers during the grinding process. The market can be divided into two main types: UV type and Non-UV type. UV type tapes utilize ultraviolet light for easier removal without leaving residue, enhancing production efficiency. In contrast, Non-UV type tapes rely on mechanical removal methods and are usually more cost-effective but may present challenges in residue management. Both types cater to diverse industry needs and manufacturing environments.</p></p>
