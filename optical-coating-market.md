<p><p>The optical coating market encompasses various specialized coatings designed to enhance performance in optical applications. Anti-Reflective (AR) coatings minimize reflection and improve transmission for lenses and displays. High Reflective coatings maximize light reflection for mirrors and lasers. Transparent Conductive coatings enable transparent electrical conductivity, crucial for touchscreens and solar cells. Filter coatings selectively transmit specific wavelengths, while beamsplitter coatings divide light into various paths. Electrodeposited (EC) coatings enhance energy efficiency by regulating light and heat transmission in smart windows.</p></p>
