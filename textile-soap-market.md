<p><strong>The Textile Soap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anion</li><li>Cation</li><li>Zwitterion</li><li>Nonionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile soap market comprises four main types based on their ionic characteristics: Anionic, Cationic, Zwitterionic, and Nonionic. Anionic soaps, typically derived from fatty acids, effectively remove dirt and stains due to their negative charge. Cationic soaps, positively charged, are often used for softening and disinfecting textiles. Zwitterionic soaps possess both negative and positive charges, offering versatility in cleaning and conditioning. Nonionic soaps, lacking a charge, are gentle on fabrics and effective in various conditions, making them suitable for delicate textiles.</p></p>
