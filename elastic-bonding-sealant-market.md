<p><p>The elastic bonding sealant market is diversified into three main types: rubber type, resin type, and oil-based type. Rubber-type sealants offer flexibility and durability for applications requiring movement and resistance to weathering. Resin-type sealants, often used for their superior adhesion and hardness, are ideal for structural applications. Oil-based sealants, known for their ease of application and moisture resistance, are popular for general-purpose sealing. Each type caters to specific performance needs in construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
