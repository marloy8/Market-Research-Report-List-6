<p><p>The mixed xylene market comprises primarily two types: solvent grade and isomer grade. In contrast, isomer grade mixed xylene focuses on the production of high-purity isomers, such as ortho-xylene, meta-xylene, and para-xylene, which are essential for manufacturing petrochemicals, plastics, and synthetic fibers. Both grades serve distinct applications, reflecting the diverse utility of mixed xylenes in various industries.</p></p>
