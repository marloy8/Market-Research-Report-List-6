Prismatic batteries feature a rectangular shape, allowing for efficient space utilization in applications like electric vehicles and consumer electronics. They are known for their high energy density and superior thermal management. In contrast, cylindrical batteries, typically round, offer robust performance, stability, and ease of manufacturing. They are widely used in portable electronics and electric vehicles due to their high discharge rates and reliability.</p></p>
