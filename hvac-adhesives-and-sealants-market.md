<p><p>The HVAC adhesives and sealants market includes hybrid adhesives and neutral cure silicone sealants, both essential for efficient system performance. Hybrid adhesives combine properties of different adhesive types, offering strong bonding capabilities and flexibility, making them ideal for various HVAC applications. Neutral cure silicone sealants, on the other hand, cure without releasing acetic acid, making them suitable for use on sensitive metals and materials. Together, they enhance system durability, efficiency, and insulation, ensuring optimal operation in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
