<p><ul><li>PLCC Socket</li><li>DIP Socket</li><li>IC Socket</li><li>LGA Socket</li><li>Transistor Socket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The connector socket market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. PLCC sockets allow for surface-mount integrated circuits, enhancing PCB space efficiency. DIP sockets accommodate dual in-line packaged components, facilitating easy replacements. IC sockets provide versatile connectivity for integrated circuits, enabling effective heat dissipation. LGA sockets are designed for processors, ensuring reliable connections with minimal signal loss. Transistor sockets offer flexibility for inserting and removing transistors without soldering, simplifying circuit modifications. Each socket type plays a crucial role in modern electronics.</p></p>
