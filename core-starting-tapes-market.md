<p><p>The Core Starting Tapes market includes two main types: single coated and double coated tapes. Single coated tapes consist of an adhesive layer on one side, ideal for applications requiring adhesion to a surface without bonding to another tape layer. In contrast, double coated tapes feature adhesive on both sides, facilitating bonding between two surfaces or materials. These tapes find applications in industries like packaging, automotive, and electronics, where strong adhesion and versatility are essential for various bonding and mounting solutions.</p></p>
