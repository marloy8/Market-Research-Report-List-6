<p><p>The IGZO display market comprises two primary types: amorphous IGZO and crystalline IGZO. Amorphous IGZO offers advantages such as lower production costs and improved flexibility, making it suitable for applications like flexible displays and large screens. In contrast, crystalline IGZO provides higher mobility and efficiency, leading to better color reproduction and response times, making it ideal for high-end displays. Both types enhance the overall performance of electronic devices, catering to diverse consumer needs in the display technology landscape.</p></p>
