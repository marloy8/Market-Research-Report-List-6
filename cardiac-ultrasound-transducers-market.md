<p><ul><li>Sector Transducer</li><li>Microconvex Transducer</li><li>Linear Transducer</li><li>Pencil Transducer</li><li>Concave Transducer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac ultrasound transducers market consists of various types designed for specific imaging needs. Sector transducers are used for Doppler imaging, offering a sector-shaped view. Microconvex transducers provide a wider field of view in tight spaces. Linear transducers excel in vascular imaging with high-resolution images. Pencil transducers, known for their compact size, are ideal for focused imaging. Concave transducers offer an extended depth of field for larger structures. Other types may include specialized or innovative designs tailored for unique cardiac assessments.</p></p>
