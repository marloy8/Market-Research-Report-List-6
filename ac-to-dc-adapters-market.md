<p><p>AC to DC adapters convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) for powering electronic devices. They are categorized by polarity: Center Positive (+) and Center Negative (-) connect types. In Center Positive adapters, the inner pin is positive, and the outer sleeve is negative. Conversely, Center Negative adapters have a negative inner pin and a positive outer sleeve. These types ensure compatibility with devices requiring specific polarity. The market for both types caters to diverse electronics, emphasizing safety and proper voltage regulation to prevent damage.</p></p>
