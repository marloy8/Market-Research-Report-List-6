<p><p>The intraoral CR (computed radiography) screen phosphor scanner market includes two main types: pointer type and digital type. Pointer type scanners utilize a traditional manual approach, where users position the phosphor plates for image capture, often relying on visual cues. In contrast, digital type scanners incorporate advanced technology for automatic image processing and enhanced image quality. These scanners streamline workflows, improve diagnostic accuracy, and are increasingly favored in modern dental practices, driving demand for both types in the market.</p></p>
