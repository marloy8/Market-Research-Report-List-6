<p><p>The commercial electric cooking equipment market includes various types such as induction hobs, commercial hotplates, and ceramic hobs. Induction hobs use electromagnetic technology to heat the cookware directly, providing fast and efficient cooking. Commercial hotplates are traditional electric cooking surfaces that offer consistent heating for various cooking tasks. Ceramic hobs use radiant heating elements underneath a smooth ceramic surface to cook food evenly and quickly. Each type of equipment caters to different cooking needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
