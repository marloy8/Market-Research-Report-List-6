<p><strong>The Corner Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halogen/Incandescent</li><li>Xenon/HID</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corner lights market includes three main types: Halogen/Incandescent, Xenon/HID, and LED. Halogen/Incandescent lights are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used but less energy-efficient. Xenon/HID lights offer brighter illumination and longer lifespan, though they are more expensive and require higher voltage. LED lights are the most advanced, providing superior energy efficiency, durability, and brightness, with a longer lifespan and lower maintenance costs. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing performance, cost, and energy efficiency in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
