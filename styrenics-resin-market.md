<p><p>Styrenics resins encompass various polymer types, with key segments including General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS), High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), Styrene-Acrylonitrile (SAN), and Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS). GPPS is known for its clarity and rigidity, whereas HIPS offers enhanced impact resistance due to the addition of rubber. SAN combines the properties of polystyrene and acrylonitrile, providing better chemical resistance. ABS is a versatile thermoplastic known for its toughness and impact resistance, making it ideal for various applications, including consumer goods and automotive components.</p></p>
