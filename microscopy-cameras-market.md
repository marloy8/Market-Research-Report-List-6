<p><p>The microscopy cameras market is divided into two main types: monochrome and color microscopy cameras. Monochrome microscopy cameras capture images in grayscale, providing high sensitivity and contrast, making them suitable for various scientific applications, including fluorescence and dark-field microscopy. In contrast, color microscopy cameras acquire images in full color, enabling detailed observation of specimens with natural color attributes, ideal for biological and material science research. Both types cater to distinct imaging requirements based on the application and desired image quality.</p></p>
