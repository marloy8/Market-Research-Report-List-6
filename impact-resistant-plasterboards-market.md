<p><p>The impact-resistant plasterboard market features two primary types: tapered-edged and square-edged boards. In contrast, square-edged plasterboards have flat edges, providing a more seamless, uniform appearance for wall applications. Both types are designed to withstand physical impacts better than standard boards, catering to commercial and residential spaces where durability and performance are essential.</p></p>
