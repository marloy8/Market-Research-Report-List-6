<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Powered Hydraulic Steering</li><li>Electric Powered Steering Systems</li><li>Hydraulic Powered Steering</li><li>Manual Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle steering market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Electric Powered Hydraulic Steering (EPHS)** integrates electric motors with hydraulic systems to enhance steering response and efficiency.</p><p>2. **Electric Powered Steering Systems (EPS)** use electric motors exclusively, offering precise control and reducing vehicle weight.</p><p>3. **Hydraulic Powered Steering** relies on hydraulic fluid pressure for assist, providing reliable performance but with heavier components.</p><p>4. **Manual Steering** is a traditional system requiring physical effort for steering input, offering simplicity and low maintenance but lacks power assistance.</p></p>
