<p><p>The biopharma buffer market comprises several types, each serving unique functions in biological applications. Phosphate buffers maintain pH stability, essential for enzyme reactions. Acetate buffers are widely used in protein purification and electrophoresis due to their compatibility with various biological systems. TRIS buffers are favored for their low reactivity and stability across a range of temperatures, often utilized in molecular biology. Other buffers include HEPES and MOPS, catering to specialized applications in research and production, enhancing overall biopharmaceutical development.</p></p>
