<p><strong>The Height Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The height sensors market is primarily divided into two types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type height sensors physically touch the object to measure its height, ensuring precise readings but potentially damaging delicate surfaces. Non-contact type height sensors utilize technologies such as laser, ultrasonic, or infrared to gauge height without physical interaction. This method offers speed and safety, particularly suited for fragile objects. Both types are essential in various applications, including manufacturing, automotive, and robotics, ensuring accurate height measurement tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
