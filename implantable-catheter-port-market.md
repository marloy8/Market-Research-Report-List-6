<p><p>The implantable catheter port market is primarily divided into two types: titanium infusion ports and plastic infusion ports. Titanium infusion ports are known for their durability, corrosion resistance, and biocompatibility, making them suitable for long-term use in patients requiring frequent venous access. In contrast, plastic infusion ports offer a lightweight and cost-effective alternative, often used for shorter treatment durations. Both types are designed to facilitate medication delivery while minimizing discomfort and improving patient quality of life during therapy.</p></p>
