<p><ul><li>Rotational Molding Plastic Inspection Chamber</li><li>Welded Piping Plastic Inspection Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic inspection chamber market is primarily divided into two types: Rotational Molding Plastic Inspection Chambers and Welded Piping Plastic Inspection Chambers. Rotational molding involves shaping plastic by rotating a mold, creating strong, seamless chambers ideal for underground applications. In contrast, welded piping plastic inspection chambers are constructed by welding plastic components together, providing robust and customizable solutions for drainage and sewer systems. Both types are used for efficient access and maintenance of underground utilities, ensuring durability and reliability.</p></p>
