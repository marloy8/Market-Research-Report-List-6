<p><ul><li>Fixed Cutter</li><li>Roller Cone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas and oil drill bits market comprises primarily two types: fixed cutter bits and roller cone bits. Fixed cutter bits feature a solid body with immovable cutting elements made from materials like diamond or tungsten carbide, making them ideal for hard rock formations. Roller cone bits, on the other hand, consist of rotating cones with sharp teeth that penetrate softer formations, providing versatile drilling capabilities. Both types are crucial in optimizing drilling efficiency and enhancing performance across various geological conditions.</p></p>
