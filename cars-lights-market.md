Halogen lights are the traditional option, known for their affordability and ease of replacement but have lower efficiency and shorter lifespans. HID lighting offers brighter illumination and better visibility, making it popular for high-end vehicles, though it is pricier and requires specialized fixtures. LED lighting is increasingly favored for its energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and customizable designs, making it a preferred choice in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
