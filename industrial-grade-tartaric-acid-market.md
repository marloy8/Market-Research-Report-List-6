<p><p>The industrial-grade tartaric acid market comprises three primary types: L-tartaric acid, D-tartaric acid, and DL-tartaric acid. L-tartaric acid, a natural enantiomer, is predominantly used in food and beverage applications for its stabilizing properties. D-tartaric acid, less common, is mainly utilized in chemical synthesis and research. DL-tartaric acid, a racemic mixture of both L and D forms, is often used in pharmaceuticals and as a resolving agent in chemistry. Together, these variants cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
