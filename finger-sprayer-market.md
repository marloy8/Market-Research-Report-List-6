<p><p>Finger sprayers are available in three main types in the market: ribbed, smooth, and others. Ribbed finger sprayers are designed with grooves or ridges on the surface of the spray head, providing a better grip for the user. Smooth finger sprayers, on the other hand, have a sleek and plain design without any texture on the spray head. The "others" category includes finger sprayers with unique features or designs that do not fit into the ribbed or smooth categories. Each type offers a different user experience and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
