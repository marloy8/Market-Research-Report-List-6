<p><p>The hydraulic lime market is categorized into three main types based on their strength and application characteristics: NHL2, NHL3.5, and NHL5. NHL2 is the weakest, suitable for internal plastering and non-load-bearing stonework. NHL3.5 offers moderate strength, making it ideal for external masonry and general construction. NHL5 is the strongest variant, designed for heavy-duty applications like structural elements and high-stress environments. These varying strengths cater to different construction needs while promoting sustainability and heritage conservation.</p></p>
