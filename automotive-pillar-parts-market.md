<p><p>The automotive pillar parts market encompasses four main types: A Pillar, B Pillar, C Pillar, and D Pillar. The A Pillar, located at the front, supports the windshield and roof, enhancing structural integrity and safety features. The B Pillar separates the front and rear doors, providing strength and support for side-impact protection. The C Pillar is found in larger vehicles, stabilizing the rear roof area. The D Pillar, typically in SUVs and larger cars, adds structural support at the rear ends.</p></p>
