<p><p>High-performance pigments (HPPs) are specialized colorants used in various applications, known for their excellent color stability, durability, and performance under extreme conditions. The HPP market is primarily divided into two types: organic HPPs and inorganic HPPs. Organic HPPs are derived from carbon-based compounds and are favored for their vibrant colors and brilliance, commonly used in coatings, plastics, and printing inks. Inorganic HPPs, derived from mineral sources, offer superior UV resistance and opacity, making them ideal for applications like automotive coatings and construction materials.</p></p>
