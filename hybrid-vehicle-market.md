<p><strong>The Hybrid Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid Vehicle</li><li>Series Hybrid Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid vehicle market is primarily divided into two types: parallel and series hybrid vehicles. In parallel hybrids, both the internal combustion engine and electric motor can power the vehicle simultaneously, optimizing fuel efficiency and performance. In contrast, series hybrids rely exclusively on the electric motor for propulsion while the engine acts solely as a generator, providing power to the electric motor and charging the battery. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, performance, and design complexity.</p></p>
