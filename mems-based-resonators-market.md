<p><p>MEMS-based resonators are integral components in various electronic devices, categorized into two main types: series resonance and parallel resonance. Series resonance type resonators operate at a frequency where the impedance is minimal, making them ideal for high-frequency applications, such as filters. In contrast, parallel resonance type resonators function at a frequency where impedance is maximal, suitable for applications requiring stability and low power consumption, such as oscillators. Each type serves distinct market needs, driving their demand across industries.</p></p>
