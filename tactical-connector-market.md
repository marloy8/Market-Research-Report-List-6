<p><p>The Tactical Connector Market is primarily categorized into two types of fiber: Single-Mode (SM) and Multi-Mode (MM) fibers. SM fibers are designed for long-distance communication, providing higher bandwidth and lower attenuation, making them ideal for military and industrial applications. In contrast, MM fibers support shorter distances, offering higher data rates over limited ranges, which makes them suitable for localized connectivity in tactical environments. Both types cater to the increasing demand for robust, reliable communication in demanding settings.</p></p>
