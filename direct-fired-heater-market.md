<p><p>Direct Fired Heater Market has three main types: Radiant Heater, Convection Heater, and Radiant-Convection Heater. Radiant heaters transfer heat directly to objects or people in their line of sight, making them ideal for spot heating. Convection heaters circulate warm air throughout a space for even heating. Radiant-Convection heaters combine the benefits of both types, providing direct heat as well as circulating warm air. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the heating requirements of the specific application.</p></p>
