In contrast, superhydrophobic coatings, exhibiting a contact angle greater than 150 degrees, create an extreme water-repelling effect, causing water droplets to bead and roll off easily. Both types find applications in various industries, including automotive, textiles, and electronics, to enhance product durability and performance.</p></p>
