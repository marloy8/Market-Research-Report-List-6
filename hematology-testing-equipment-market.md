<p><p>Hematology testing equipment encompasses various technologies critical for blood analysis. Hematology analyzers automate the complete blood count process, delivering precise cell counts and indices. Flow cytometers allow detailed cellular analysis and immunophenotyping by using laser technology to measure physical and chemical characteristics of cells. Coagulation analyzers assess the blood's clotting functions, essential for diagnosing bleeding disorders. Slide stainers facilitate the preparation of blood smears by applying stains, enhancing cell visibility for microscopic examination and accurate diagnosis.</p></p>
