<p><p>Bioabsorbable scaffolds are advanced medical devices designed for use in tissue engineering and regenerative medicine. These scaffolds support cell attachment and growth while gradually degrading over time, ultimately being absorbed by the body. They are mainly used in orthopedic surgeries, cardiovascular procedures, and wound healing, offering the advantage of eliminating the need for surgical removal.</p><p>The Bioabsorbable Scaffold Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.90% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries, and advancements in biomaterials technology. Regulatory approvals for innovative bioabsorbable products are also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Latest trends in the market include a focus on personalized medicine, the use of 3D printing technologies to create customized scaffolds, and integration with drug delivery systems. Additionally, research into novel materials such as polymers and composites is enhancing scaffold performance and applications. The growing interest in stem cell therapies and bioprinting is expected to further boost the market, as these technologies complement the functionalities offered by bioabsorbable scaffolds in tissue regeneration and repair.</p></p>
