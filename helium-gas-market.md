<p><ul><li>Liquid Helium</li><li>Gaseous Helium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helium gas market can be categorized into two primary types: liquid helium and gaseous helium. Liquid helium is utilized in cryogenics, particularly for cooling superconducting magnets and in MRI machines, due to its low boiling point. Gaseous helium, on the other hand, is commonly used in various applications such as inflating balloons, providing inert environments for welding, and as a lifting gas in lighter-than-air crafts. Each market type serves distinct industrial and scientific needs, driving demand accordingly.</p></p>
