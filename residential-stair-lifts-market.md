<p><ul><li>Straight Stairlift</li><li>Curved Stairlift</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential stair lift market is primarily divided into two types: straight stairlifts and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for straight staircases, featuring a simpler installation and lower cost, making them ideal for most standard homes. In contrast, curved stairlifts are customized to fit intricate stair designs, accommodating bends and landings, which often results in higher costs and more complex installations. Both types enhance mobility, providing safe and efficient access for individuals with limited mobility in their homes.</p></p>
