<p><p>The Port Ball Valves market is divided into two main types: Soft Seal Valves and Hard Seal Valves. Soft Seal Valves use elastomeric materials for sealing, providing excellent leak-tight performance and flexibility, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. In contrast, Hard Seal Valves feature rigid materials like metal or ceramic for sealing, offering high durability and resistance to harsh conditions, ideal for high-pressure environments. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
