<p><strong>The Extruded Baked Snacks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Extrusion</li><li>Cold Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extruded baked snacks market is primarily categorized into hot and cold extrusion types. Hot extrusion involves heating raw materials to create a dough that is then forced through a die, resulting in a crunchy texture ideal for snacks like cheese puffs and potato chips. Cold extrusion, on the other hand, operates at lower temperatures, maintaining the integrity of sensitive ingredients, which is suitable for producing textured or shaped snacks that require a softer consistency. Both methods offer unique textures and flavors, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
