<p><strong>The Mist Eliminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-mesh Mist Eliminators</li><li>Baffle-type Mist Climinators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mist eliminator market comprises primarily two types: wire-mesh mist eliminators and baffle-type mist eliminators. Wire-mesh mist eliminators utilize a mesh structure to capture and coalesce mist droplets from vapor streams, effectively reducing emissions in industrial processes. Baffle-type mist eliminators feature angled plates that redirect mist-laden gases, allowing droplets to settle and be removed. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing air quality and improving efficiency in various applications, including chemical processing, power generation, and oil refining.</p></p>
