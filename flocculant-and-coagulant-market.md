<p><p>Flocculants and coagulants are chemical agents used in water treatment processes to facilitate the removal of suspended solids. Coagulants, such as aluminum sulfate, work by neutralizing charges on particles, allowing them to clump together into larger aggregates, or flocs. Flocculants, typically polymers, enhance this process by further binding these aggregates together, promoting sedimentation and clarification in various applications, including municipal water treatment, wastewater management, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Flocculant and Coagulant Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.60% during the forecast period.
