<p><p>Low-pressure casting machines are specialized equipment used in the manufacturing process of metal parts, particularly those requiring high precision and quality. These machines operate by introducing molten metal into a mold cavity under controlled low pressure, which enhances the flow of the material and reduces defects. This method is particularly beneficial for creating complex geometries and improving the mechanical properties of cast components, commonly used in the automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods sectors.</p><p>The Low-Pressure Casting Machines Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period.
