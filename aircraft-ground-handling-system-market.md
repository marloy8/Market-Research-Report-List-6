<p><p>The Aircraft Ground-Handling System market comprises three main types: Conventional, Electrical, and Hybrid Systems. Conventional systems rely on traditional fuel-powered equipment like tugs and loaders, offering reliability but higher emissions. Electrical systems use battery-powered equipment, reducing emissions and noise, ideal for eco-friendly operations. Hybrid systems combine fuel and electric power, balancing efficiency and sustainability. Each type caters to varying operational needs, with airports increasingly adopting electrical and hybrid systems to meet environmental regulations and enhance operational efficiency while maintaining performance standards.</p></p>
