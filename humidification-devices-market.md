<p><strong>The Humidification Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humidification devices market primarily comprises vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers work by heating water to create steam, which is then released into the air to increase humidity levels. In contrast, water spray humidifiers utilize a nozzle to disperse fine water droplets into the air, effectively humidifying the space without heating. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with vapor humidifiers typically offering higher humidity levels and water spray humidifiers being energy-efficient and easier to maintain.</p></p>
