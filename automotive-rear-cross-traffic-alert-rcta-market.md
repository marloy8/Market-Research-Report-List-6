<p><p>Automotive Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA) is a safety feature designed to assist drivers when reversing out of parking spaces or driveways. This technology is particularly useful in crowded parking lots or areas with limited visibility, reducing the risk of collisions.</p><p>The Automotive Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA) Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing consumer demand for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and heightened awareness of vehicle safety.
