<p><strong>The Dental Liners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Ionomer</li><li>Calcium Hydroxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental liners market primarily consists of two key types: glass ionomer and calcium hydroxide. Glass ionomer liners are favored for their ability to chemically bond to teeth, providing a protective seal and releasing fluoride, which aids in preventing decay. Calcium hydroxide liners are known for their pulp-capping properties, promoting dentin regeneration and offering excellent antimicrobial effects. Both materials play crucial roles in restorative dentistry, enhancing tooth protection and supporting overall dental health.</p></p>
