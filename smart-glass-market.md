<p><strong>The Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Glass market comprises three main types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD (Suspended Particle Device) Smart Glass, and PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) Smart Glass. Electrochromic Smart Glass changes transparency using electrical currents, providing shading and privacy. SPD Smart Glass uses suspended particles that align to control light and glare, allowing for variable transparency. PDLC Smart Glass switches between transparent and opaque states when voltage is applied, offering instant privacy and light control for various applications in architecture and automotive industries.</p></p>
