<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Wood Chipper</li><li>PTO Wood Chipper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood chippers market can be divided into two main types: self-propelled wood chippers and PTO wood chippers. Self-propelled wood chippers are equipped with their own engine and wheels, allowing them to be easily transported around a property. PTO wood chippers, on the other hand, are powered by a vehicle's power take-off system. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications, such as landscaping, agriculture, forestry, and construction. Both types play a crucial role in processing wood waste efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
