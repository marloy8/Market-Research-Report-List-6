<p><p>Lab Automation refers to the use of technology to streamline and automate various processes in the laboratory. Task Targeted Automation (TTA) focuses on automating specific tasks within the lab, such as sample preparation or analysis. Total Laboratory Automation (TLA) incorporates a complete automation system that connects multiple instruments and processes, allowing for a fully automated laboratory operation. TTA is suitable for labs that want to automate specific tasks, while TLA is ideal for labs looking to automate their entire workflow for increased efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
