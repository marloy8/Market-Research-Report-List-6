<p><ul><li>Unit Type Protection</li><li>Non Unit Type Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Overcurrent Relays market can be classified into two main types: Unit Type Protection and Non-Unit Type Protection. Unit Type Protection focuses on safeguarding specific equipment by directly monitoring the current flowing through it, allowing for precise trip settings based on defined thresholds. In contrast, Non-Unit Type Protection employs a broader approach, assessing overall system currents without isolating individual devices, offering a more generalized level of protection. Both types play critical roles in ensuring electrical system reliability and safety.</p></p>
