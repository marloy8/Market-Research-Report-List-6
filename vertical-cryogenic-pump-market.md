<p><strong>The Vertical Cryogenic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Non-Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical cryogenic pump market is primarily divided into two types: submersible and non-submersible pumps. Submersible cryogenic pumps are designed to operate while submerged in the cryogenic fluid, allowing for efficient fluid transfer with reduced vaporization. Non-submersible pumps, on the other hand, are located above the fluid level and utilize a suction mechanism to draw the cryogenic liquid into the system. Both types are crucial for applications in industries like aerospace, LNG processing, and chemical manufacturing, each offering unique advantages based on operational needs.</p></p>
