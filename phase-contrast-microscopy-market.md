<p><strong>The Phase Contrast Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Phase-Contrast Microscopy</li><li>Quantitative Phase-Contrast Microscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phase contrast microscopy market is divided into two main types: traditional phase-contrast microscopy and quantitative phase-contrast microscopy. Traditional phase-contrast microscopy enhances the visibility of transparent specimens by converting phase shifts in light into brightness differences, making it ideal for observing living cells. In contrast, quantitative phase-contrast microscopy provides detailed quantitative data on cell morphology and dynamics by measuring phase shifts more accurately, allowing for enhanced analysis of cellular processes without staining or labeling, thus preserving the natural state of specimens.</p></p>
