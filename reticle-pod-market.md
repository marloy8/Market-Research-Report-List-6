In contrast, the Non-EUV market encompasses traditional photolithography technologies, employing longer wavelengths suitable for less complex semiconductor features. Both markets cater to various applications, with EUV targeting high-performance and smaller device nodes, while Non-EUV serves established processes and larger nodes in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
