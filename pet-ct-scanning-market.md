<p><p>PET-CT scanning, or Positron Emission Tomography-Computed Tomography, is an advanced imaging technique that combines metabolic and structural information to enhance diagnostic accuracy in various medical conditions, particularly cancer. The market is also benefitting from the integration of artificial intelligence, which enhances image interpretation and diagnostic capabilities. </p><p>Furthermore, an aging population and the growing demand for personalized medicine are contributing to the expansion of this market. Key trends include the development of portable PET-CT devices, increased applications beyond oncology (such as neurology), and enhancements in radiotracers for more accurate imaging.</p><p>The PET-CT scanning market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period, indicating robust demand for this essential diagnostic tool and its continuing evolution within the healthcare landscape.</p></p>
