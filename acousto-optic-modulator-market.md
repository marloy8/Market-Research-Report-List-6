<p><strong>The Acousto-Optic Modulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Modulators</li><li>Free-Space Acousto-Optic Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acousto-optic modulator market is divided into two main types: fiber-coupled and free-space acousto-optic modulators. Fiber-coupled acousto-optic modulators are designed to integrate seamlessly with optical fibers, enabling precise control of light in fiber-optic systems, making them ideal for telecommunications and medical applications. In contrast, free-space acousto-optic modulators operate independently of optical fibers, utilizing air as the transmission medium, and are commonly used in laser scanning, imaging systems, and industrial laser applications, offering flexibility in alignment and setup.</p></p>
