<p><p>Outdoor fire protection coatings are essential for safeguarding structures against fire hazards. The market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions, easier application, and straightforward cleanup. They are ideal for environmentally conscious applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings contain organic solvents, providing robust adhesion and durability but often have higher VOC levels. Each type serves specific needs based on environmental considerations and performance requirements.</p></p>
