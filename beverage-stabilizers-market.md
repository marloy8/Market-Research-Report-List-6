<p><p>The beverage stabilizers market encompasses natural and synthetic substances that enhance the texture, consistency, and shelf life of beverages. Xanthan gum is a microbial polysaccharide used for its thickening properties. Carboxymethyl cellulose is a cellulose derivative that offers viscosity and stabilization. Carrageenan, derived from seaweed, provides gelling and thickening benefits, particularly in dairy and non-dairy drinks. Gum Arabic, a natural gum from acacia trees, serves as an emulsifier and stabilizer, enabling a balanced formulation in various beverages.</p></p>
