<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Interference Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Pass Type</li><li>Low-Pass Type</li><li>Bandpass Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interference filters are optical devices that selectively transmit specific wavelengths of light while blocking others. In the market, there are three main types: </p><p>1. **High-Pass Filters** allow high wavelengths to pass while blocking lower ones, used in applications needing light separation.</p><p>2. **Low-Pass Filters** permit lower wavelengths, filtering out higher wavelengths, ideal for protecting sensors or enhancing contrast in visual systems.</p><p>3. **Bandpass Filters** transmit a specific range of wavelengths, useful in spectroscopy and communications, enabling precise control over light filtering for various applications.</p></p>
