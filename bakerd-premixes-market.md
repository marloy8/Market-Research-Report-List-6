<p><p>Bakery premixes can be categorized into three main types: Complete mixes, Dough-base mixes, and Dough concentrates. Complete mixes offer all necessary ingredients for baking, requiring only water and sometimes additional components. Dough-base mixes provide a foundation for dough, needing additional ingredients like fats or flavorings for customization. Dough concentrates are highly refined products that require significant water to create dough, allowing bakers greater flexibility in formulation while reducing preparation time. Each type serves distinct needs within the baking industry.</p></p>
