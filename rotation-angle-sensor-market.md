<p><ul><li>Brushless Sensor</li><li>Brushed Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotation angle sensor market is primarily divided into brushless and brushed sensors. Brushless sensors utilize electronic mechanisms to detect rotation, offering advantages such as longer lifespan, lower maintenance, and reduced electromagnetic interference. In contrast, brushed sensors rely on physical brushes to make contact with the rotating elements, which may lead to wear over time but are often simpler and more cost-effective for specific applications. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, robotics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
