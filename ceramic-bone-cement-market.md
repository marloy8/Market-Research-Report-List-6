<p><p>The ceramic bone cement market is categorized into low, medium, and high viscosity cements, depending on their flow characteristics and application suitability. Low viscosity cements are designed for precise application and improved penetration into bone structures, making them ideal for delicate procedures. Medium viscosity cements balance flow and setting time, suitable for standard orthopedic applications. High viscosity cements, characterized by their thicker consistency, provide enhanced stability and support in load-bearing areas, making them ideal for high-stress environments.</p></p>
