<p><ul><li>Swipe Sensors</li><li>Area Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensors market is primarily divided into two types: swipe sensors and area sensors. </p><p>Swipe sensors require users to slide a finger across the surface to capture the fingerprint, making them compact and cost-effective. In contrast, area sensors capture the entire fingerprint in one scan, providing higher accuracy and speed with larger sensing areas. These sensors are often used in smartphones, security systems, and access control devices, catering to diverse applications and consumer demands in biometric technology.</p></p>
