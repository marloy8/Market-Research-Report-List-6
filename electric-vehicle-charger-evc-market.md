<p><ul><li>On-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC)</li><li>Off-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC) market is divided into **On-board** and **Off-board** types. **On-board EVCs** are integrated into the vehicle, converting AC power from charging stations into DC to charge the battery. They are compact and suitable for slower charging. **Off-board EVCs** are external, high-power chargers that directly supply DC power to the vehicle, enabling faster charging. On-board chargers are common for home use, while off-board chargers are ideal for public or commercial fast-charging stations. Both types cater to diverse EV charging needs.</p></p>
