<p><ul><li>Buried Photodiode-structure</li><li>Selection Transistor</li><li>MOS Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Pixel Sensor (APS) market includes three main types: Buried Photodiode-structure, Selection Transistor, and MOS Amplifier. The Buried Photodiode-structure enhances light absorption and reduces noise, leading to improved image quality. The Selection Transistor allows for individual pixel readout, increasing operational flexibility and control in image processing. The MOS Amplifier integrates amplification within each pixel, minimizing signal loss and enhancing sensitivity. Together, these technologies drive advancements in imaging applications across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
