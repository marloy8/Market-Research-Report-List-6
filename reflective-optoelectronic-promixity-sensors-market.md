<p><p>The Reflective Optoelectronic Proximity Sensors market includes two primary types: IR Reflective and LED Reflective sensors. IR Reflective Optoelectronic Proximity Sensors utilize infrared light to detect nearby objects by measuring the reflection of emitted light. These sensors are often used in applications where low visibility is needed. In contrast, LED Reflective Optoelectronic Proximity Sensors employ visible light for detection, providing clear signaling and enhanced visibility. Both types are widely used in automation, safety systems, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
