<p><p>The adjustable power hospital bed market primarily consists of two types: full electric and semi-electric hospital beds. Full electric beds allow users to adjust the head, foot, and height positions using a remote control, offering maximum flexibility for patient comfort and medical care. In contrast, semi-electric beds combine electric adjustments for the head and foot sections with manual adjustments for height, providing a balance between ease of use and cost-effectiveness. Both types enhance patient care and facilitate nursing tasks.</p></p>
