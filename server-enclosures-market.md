<p><p>Server enclosures are classified into two main types: wall-mounted and detached. Wall-mounted enclosures are designed to optimize space in smaller environments, ideal for offices or compact data centers. They securely hold servers and networking equipment while promoting airflow and accessibility. Detached enclosures, on the other hand, are standalone units that allow for greater scalability and cooling capabilities. These are suitable for larger data centers where more equipment and additional features such as higher power and cooling solutions are needed.</p></p>
