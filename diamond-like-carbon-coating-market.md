<p><p>The Diamond-Like Carbon (DLC) coating market comprises two primary types: Hydrogen-Free DLC and Hydrogenated DLC. Hydrogen-Free DLC is characterized by its greater hardness and wear resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications like cutting tools and tribological coatings. In contrast, Hydrogenated DLC contains hydrogen, which imparts enhanced flexibility and adhesion properties, ideal for applications in electronics and optics. Each type addresses different performance needs, catering to various industries seeking advanced surface protection solutions.</p></p>
