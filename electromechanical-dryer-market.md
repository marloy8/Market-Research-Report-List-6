<p><p>The electromechanical dryer market is segmented into two primary types: electro dryers and mechanical dryers. Electro dryers utilize electrical energy to generate heat, enhancing efficiency and control over drying processes, making them suitable for sensitive materials. Mechanical dryers, on the other hand, rely on mechanical means, such as rotation or agitation, to facilitate drying, often found in bulk operations. Both types cater to various industries, including agriculture, textiles, and food processing, addressing diverse drying needs based on material properties and production scales.</p></p>
